About

I am a doctoral candidate at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. My primary research is in computational geometry, with additional work in human-robot interaction and numerical linear algebra. My work in computational geometry focuses on the design and analysis of algorithms for enumerating non-crossing combinatorial structures.
